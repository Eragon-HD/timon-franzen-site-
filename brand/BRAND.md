# Timon Franzen — brand

## The mark

`bird-mark.svg` — a bird in flight, reduced to what matters.

- **Seven feathers per wing**, fanned into an S-curve: motion, momentum, forward.
- **Weight hierarchy** — the leading feather is bold (5.6), each one behind it
  quieter (down to 2.4). Engraved, crest-like: old money discipline.
- **A fine seal ring** (1.6) that the wings break through: heritage that
  doesn't contain ambition. The young-entrepreneur part.
- **One colour** (`currentColor` — inherits its surroundings), no fill, no
  gradients in the mark itself. Confident enough to whisper.

Character in one line: *old money posture, young hunger — confident, quiet.*

## Usage

- Works on dark and light; give it room — clear space of at least half the
  ring's radius on all sides.
- At 16 px (favicon): drop the ring, keep 3 feathers per wing.
- Wordmark pairing: BRICOLAGE GROTESQUE, letterspaced caps, under or right of
  the mark. Never inside the ring.
- The AI-rendered 3D bird images are **portraits, not logos** — use them for
  profile pictures and hero art only.

## Mission

Give Swiss small businesses and independents a web presence and intelligent
tools they're proud of — hand-built, fast, distinctive, with no bloat and
nothing off the shelf. Every project ships with the discipline of a
competitive athlete: relentless, focused work until it's right.
